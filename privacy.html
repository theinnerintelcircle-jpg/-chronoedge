Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ChronoEdge collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to ChronoEdge, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — collected at checkout via Stripe
  • Payment information — processed and stored securely by Stripe. ChronoEdge does not store card details
  • Telegram username — when you join our private channel
  • Subscription information — plan type, billing dates, subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Deliver alerts to your Telegram account
  • Send service-related communications such as billing notifications
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your data for marketing purposes, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Third Party Services

ChronoEdge uses the following third party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy
  • Telegram — alert delivery. Subject to Telegram's privacy policy
  • Supabase — database infrastructure. Data stored in the EU
  • Vercel — hosting infrastructure

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your subscription is active. Upon cancellation, your data is retained for 90 days and then deleted, except where we are required to retain it for legal or accounting purposes.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@chronoedge.net

6. Cookies

ChronoEdge does not use tracking cookies or third party analytics on our website.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe and is PCI DSS compliant.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify subscribers of any significant changes by email.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at hello@chronoedge.net
