How it works

Three steps. One edge.

ChronoEdge monitors every major UK watch marketplace continuously. Every new listing is captured within minutes of going live — before most buyers even open their browser.

II

We score every listing

Each listing is compared against our real market value database built from actual recent sales. We calculate the discount, the spread, and the opportunity — automatically.

When a listing hits your threshold, an alert fires instantly to your Telegram. You see it before the market does. That's the edge.

FAQ

Common questions.

How do I receive alerts?
Alerts are delivered instantly via Telegram. After subscribing you'll receive a welcome message with a link to join the private ChronoEdge channel. No app downloads needed beyond Telegram.
How accurate are the market values?
Market values are calculated from real recent sales data — not estimates or guesses. The database updates automatically every 30 minutes, so values reflect what watches are actually selling for right now.
Which watches do you track?
ChronoEdge tracks 46 specific references across Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Richard Mille, and Cartier — covering the most sought-after models in the UK grey market.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal — no questions asked, no notice period required. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
I lost access to the Telegram group — what do I do?
Email us at support@chronoedge.net and we'll restore your access within a few hours.
Is ChronoEdge legitimate?
Yes. ChronoEdge is a UK-based watch intelligence service. We use official marketplace APIs to monitor listings and deliver real deal alerts to subscribers. Questions? Email hello@chronoedge.net.
